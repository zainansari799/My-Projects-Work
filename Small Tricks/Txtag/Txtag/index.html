How Toll Tag Works

Toll Tag makes paying tolls simple and fast.There's no stopping or slowing down to pay, no waiting in line at a toll booth, and no searching for change on toll roads across Texas. The thin Toll Tag sticker goes on the inside of your windshield behind your rearview mirror. An electronic reader above the toll roads sends a signal to the microchip inside the sticker, and tolls are automatically deducted from your prepaid Toll Tag account........

How To Pay Tolls

With a Toll Tag account your tolls are prepaid. You will need to keep enough money in your account to pay for your tolls. You can add money to your account when the balance gets low, or you can sign up for AutoPay (automatic replenishment), so you never have to worry about how much money is in your TxTag account You can pay your toll by using autopay, by online or phone ,email and by in person etc.........
